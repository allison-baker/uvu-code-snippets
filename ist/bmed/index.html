B.S. in Business/Marketing Education

122 Credits to Graduate

The Business/Marketing Education curriculum prepares students to teach business, marketing, and information technology in secondary schools. Understand the role of continuous interaction with customers, the impact of marketing programs, and the ability to use information to make knowledgeable decisions.
